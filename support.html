Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add the bot to my server?

Click the "Add to Discord" button on the homepage and follow the authorization instructions. You need to have "Manage Server" permissions to add the bot.

How do I use the dashboard?

Go to the dashboard page and login with your Discord account. Once logged in, you can manage all your server settings from an easy-to-use web interface.

What are the premium features?

Premium features include advanced moderation tools, custom commands, priority support, and more. See the Premium Page for full details.

Is the bot free to use?

Yes, the bot is completely free to use with basic features. Premium features are available for a small monthly fee to support development.

How do I report a bug?

Please join our support server and describe the issue in the #bug-reports channel. Our team will investigate promptly.
