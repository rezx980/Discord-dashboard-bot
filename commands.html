Bot Commands

Moderation

  • !kick @user [reason] - Kicks a user from the server.
  • !ban @user [reason] - Bans a user from the server.
  • !mute @user [duration] - Mutes a user.
  • !warn @user [reason] - Warns a user.

Economy (Optional)

  • !balance - Checks your balance.
  • !deposit [amount] - Deposits money into your account.
  • !withdraw [amount] - Withdraws money from your account.
  • !give @user [amount] - Gives money to another user.

General

  • !ping - Checks the bot's latency.
  • !help - Displays a list of commands.
  • !serverinfo - Displays information about the server.
  • !userinfo @user - Displays information about a user.
